# Extracting Video Outline Prompt

## Input Format

You will receive a JSON input:

{ "text": "{Video Transcription Text Fragment}"

}
- The `text` field is a fragment of the video transcription content;

- This content has been manually proofread to ensure basic semantic coherence;

- Please use the input content as the sole source of information, without any external expansion, common-sense reasoning, or logical additions.

## Core Task: Balancing In-Depth Viewpoints and Critical Value

Your task is to **fully cover** all the important viewpoints and arguments in the input text, while maintaining **appropriate conciseness**, avoiding over-elaboration. The goal is to extract **8-15 core topics**, each corresponding to **3-8 minutes of video content**.

## Topic Extraction Principles

### 1. Prioritize Value of Viewpoints

- **Prioritize Uniqueness:** Prioritize content with unique perspectives, novel viewpoints, or in-depth thinking.

- **Focus on Controversy:** Focus on controversial viewpoints that are worthy of discussion and can stimulate critical thinking.

- **Logical Completeness:** Ensure that each viewpoint contains a complete logical argument (viewpoint-evidence-conclusion).

### 2. Depth of Critical Thinking Principle

- **Multidimensional Analysis:** Prioritize in-depth discussions that include multi-angle analysis and contrasting arguments.

- **Critical Thinking:** Focus on content that demonstrates critical thinking and rational analysis.

- **Value Judgment:** Highlight viewpoints that include clear value stances and judgment standards.

### 3. Social Significance Principle

- **Relevance to Reality:** Prioritize viewpoints related to current social phenomena and hot topics.

- **Universal Value:** Focus on discussions of viewpoints with universal significance and the ability to resonate with the audience.

- **Inspirational Value:** Highlight content that inspires the audience and expands their thinking.

### 4. Completeness of Expression Principle

- **Clear Viewpoints:** Ensure each topic contains a clear viewpoint and attitude.

**Sufficient Argumentation:** Guarantee the integrity of the argumentation process, including multiple arguments such as facts, logic, and emotion.

**Clear Conclusions:** Ensure the expression of viewpoints has a clear conclusion or value judgment.

## Topic Merging and Splitting Strategies

### Merging Conditions (The following should be merged into one topic):

- Discussing different levels or angles of the same social phenomenon

- Interrelated chains of argumentation

- Different expressions of viewpoints within the same value system

- Multi-dimensional analysis of the same issue

### Splitting Conditions (The following should be split into different topics):

- A fundamental shift in viewpoint stance

- A shift in the discussion topic from one area to another

- A shift in argumentation style from rational analysis to emotional expression

- A significant change in value judgment standards

## Output Format

Please strictly adhere to the following output format, without adding any additional descriptive text:

- [Subtopic 2: Highlighting the Uniqueness and Speculative Value of Viewpoints]

- …

2. **[First-Level Topic Name]**

- [Subtopic 1]

- …

## Special Considerations for Viewpoint Content

### 1. Handling Controversial Viewpoints:

- Ensure the complete and unbiased expression of controversial viewpoints.

- Include the argumentation process from different perspectives.

- Maintain the original context and emotional tone of the viewpoint expression.

### 2. Value Judgment Content:

- Clearly distinguish between factual statements and value judgments.

- Retain a clear expression of the value stance.

- Include the basis and standards for the value judgment.

### 3. Social Criticism Content:

- Maintain the constructive and rational nature of the criticism.

- Include problem analysis and solution approaches.

- Avoid purely emotional venting or meaningless complaints.

## Notes:

1. **Viewpoint Completeness**: Ensure the extracted topics fully reflect the core stance of the original viewpoint.

2. **Critical Thinking Value**: Prioritize extracting content with critical thinking value that can stimulate in-depth reflection.

3. **Social Significance**: Focus on viewpoints that offer unique insights into social phenomena and are thought-provoking.

4. **Logical Clarity**: Ensure the logical chain of viewpoint expression is complete and the argumentation process is clear.

5. **Value Neutrality**: Maintain a neutral stance during the extraction process and refrain from making value judgments on the viewpoints.
